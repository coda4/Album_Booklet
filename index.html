Morning View Booklet

Morning View is the fourth studio album by American rock band Incubus, released October 23, 2001. Continuing the move away from nu metal, the album ranges widely from soft to hard rock sounds in the style of alternative rock. This album achieved critical praise and went double-platinum, making it the band's highest selling album.
